Burna Boy Releases 6th Studio Album ‘Love, Damini’ | Listen

Finally, Burna Boy’s highly anticipated sixth studio album is out now!

  • By
    • Peter Okhide
  • July 4, 2022 | 09:51 PM

The Grammy-award winning act will have international stars like J Hus, Ed Sheeran, and a host of others on his upcoming album.

Burna Boy

Iconic Nigerian singer, songwriter and performer, Damini Ogulu, popularly known as Burna Boy, has unveiled the official tracklist for his upcoming album ‘Love, Damini’.

Advertisement

The Afrobeats poster-boy is expected to release his seventh studio album, which is scheduled for release on Friday, July 8th, 2022.
Having dropped ‘Kilometer’ and ‘Last Last‘ off the upcoming project, and the earlier announcement of ‘For My Hand which features Ed Sheeran’ , the mega superstar, whose birthday was yesterday, July 2nd, 2022, decided to share the official tracklist on the same day.
The highly-anticipated 19-track album will also feature heavyweights like J Hus, Victony, Blxst, Popcaan, Ladysmith Black Mambazo, Khelani, Khalid, and J Balvin, covering a total of four continents and six countries. We definitely can’t wait!
